Kaleido is an interactive installation featuring a custom controller that allows manipulation of a kaleidoscopic live video feed projected onto a large surface. Using Vuo, it takes the input of a camera and the custom controller, which acts as a midi, to control different variables of the kaleidoscope. The controller and slider tops were CAD modeled and 3D printed to house the electronics.
