This piece featured a watery landscape projected down onto a sheet that could be laid under to interact with. When the sheet is pushed, it affects the ripples of the water and the soundscape which swirled around 4 surrounding speakers.

Reflective coins could be skipped around the pond to introduce more visual elements and the work could be interacted with from underneath or above by many people at once.

Made in collaboration with a group, my focus was the iterative design of the interactions, coding in TouchDesigner, and building the physical installation.
