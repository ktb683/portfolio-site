Stardust is a space-themed 3–4 player strategy board game that is played in roughly 1–2 hours.

The player pieces were designed in CAD, then 3D printed or lasercut and carefully painted. The board went through an iterative design process, with the final version produced in lasercut clear acrylic.

A rotating center piece is integrated into the board as part of the gameplay, allowing players to simulate shifting orbits in space as they move through the game.
