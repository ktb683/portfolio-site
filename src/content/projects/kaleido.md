An interactive kaleidoscope installation that responds to user input through physical controls. The piece combines 3D printed housings with electronic components and custom software to generate real-time kaleidoscopic visuals.

The project explores the intersection of traditional optical toys and modern digital art, creating an accessible yet deeply engaging interactive experience.
