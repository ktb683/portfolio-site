Stranded Foundations is a mural painted by a collective and features a design generated from custom code. The flowing pattern is inspired by natural formations such as wood grain, topographical maps, and ripples on water.

The mural is installed in Space 21 at Aalto University, where the coded visuals and hand-painted execution come together to create an immersive, room-scale artwork.
